Welcome to the character creation!
You can access the help screen by pressing <F1>

First you must choose your name and your appearence.
Then press next.

Secondly you must choose your difficulty.
This decides your starting health, gold, score and inventory.
Then press next.

Thirdly you can choose your goals for the game.
Choose a goal type from the drop-down list.
Type in a valid value for the goal, then press "add goal".
You can undo adding goals by pressing "Undo last goal".
Then press next.

Lastly there is the summary screen.
Here you can review your choices.
If all mandatory information has been filled in, you can start the game.
Press next.
